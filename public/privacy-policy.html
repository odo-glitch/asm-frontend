Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

Odo Social Media - A product of Odo Marketing

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Odo Social Media ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service to manage your social media accounts.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner.

2. Data We Access and Why

When you connect your social media accounts, we request specific permissions to provide our services:

Facebook Permissions

pages_show_list
  • What: Access to the list of Facebook Pages you manage
  • Why: To allow you to select which Page to manage through our app
  • Data Stored: Page name, Page ID, profile picture URL
pages_read_engagement
  • What: Read posts, comments, and engagement metrics from your Page
  • Why: To display analytics, comments, and engagement data in your dashboard
  • Data Stored: Post content, engagement statistics (likes, comments, shares, reach)
pages_manage_posts
  • What: Create, edit, and schedule posts on your Facebook Page
  • Why: To enable you to publish and schedule content from our platform
  • Data Stored: Scheduled posts, post drafts, media files
pages_read_user_content
  • What: Read content posted by visitors to your Page
  • Why: To display customer messages and comments in our unified inbox
  • Data Stored: Visitor posts, comments, customer messages (temporarily cached)
pages_messaging
  • What: Read and send Messenger messages on behalf of your Facebook Page
  • Why: To manage customer conversations through our unified inbox
  • Data Stored: Message content (temporarily cached for display), conversation metadata
pages_utility_messaging
  • What: Send automated utility messages (appointment reminders, order updates)
  • Why: To send transactional notifications to customers who initiated conversations
  • Data Stored: Message templates, delivery status
pages_manage_metadata
  • What: Subscribe to webhooks for real-time Page activity updates
  • Why: To receive instant notifications about new messages and comments without polling
  • Data Stored: Webhook subscription configuration
pages_manage_engagement
  • What: Create, edit, and delete comments and reactions on Page posts
  • Why: To allow you to respond to comments and moderate your Page directly from our app
  • Data Stored: Comment responses (copies of what you post)
business_management
  • What: Access to Facebook Business Manager assets and Pages
  • Why: For agencies managing multiple client Pages through Business Manager
  • Data Stored: Business account structure, associated Pages and assets
public_profile (Default)
  • What: Basic profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Why: For user authentication and displaying your identity in the app
  • Data Stored: Your name, profile picture, user ID
email (Default)
  • What: Your primary email address
  • Why: For account creation, notifications, and account recovery
  • Data Stored: Email address

Instagram Permissions

instagram_basic
  • What: Basic access to your Instagram Business/Creator account
  • Why: Required to connect your Instagram account and access media
  • Data Stored: Account name, username, profile picture, Instagram account ID
instagram_manage_messages
  • What: Read and send Instagram Direct Messages
  • Why: To manage customer conversations through our unified inbox
  • Data Stored: Message content (temporarily cached), conversation metadata
instagram_manage_comments
  • What: Read and respond to comments on your Instagram posts
  • Why: To allow you to manage engagement and customer interactions from our dashboard
  • Data Stored: Comment text, replies, commenter information
instagram_content_publish
  • What: Create and publish photos, videos, and stories to Instagram
  • Why: To enable post scheduling and automated publishing
  • Data Stored: Scheduled posts, media files, captions, hashtags
instagram_manage_insights
  • What: Access Instagram analytics and insights data
  • Why: To display performance metrics in our Analytics dashboard
  • Data Stored: Engagement metrics, follower demographics, post insights

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect exclusively to provide you with our social media management services:

  • Display: Show your social media content, analytics, and messages in our dashboard
  • Scheduling: Store and publish your scheduled posts at the times you specify
  • Analytics: Calculate and display engagement metrics for your content
  • Communication: Enable you to respond to messages and comments from customers

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Sell your data to third parties
  • ❌ Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • ❌ Share your data with other users or external services
  • ❌ Post content without your explicit instruction

4. Data Storage and Security

Where We Store Data:

  • All data is stored securely in encrypted databases (Supabase)
  • Access tokens are encrypted using industry-standard AES-256 encryption
  • Data is stored on servers with enterprise-grade security measures

How Long We Keep Data:

  • Access Tokens: Stored securely until you disconnect your account
  • Scheduled Posts: Deleted after successful publication
  • Messages: Cached temporarily for display (not permanently stored)
  • Analytics Data: Retained for historical reporting purposes

Security Measures:

  • All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access tokens are encrypted at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Role-based access control for data protection

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all data we store about your social media accounts through the dashboard
  • Disconnect: Remove any connected account at any time from Settings
  • Delete: Request complete deletion of your data by contacting support
  • Export: Request a copy of your data at any time

6. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following services to provide our functionality:

  • Facebook/Meta: For accessing Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts
  • Supabase: Secure database and authentication services
  • Vercel/Render: Application hosting infrastructure

These services have their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for these services to function.

7. Data Deletion

When you disconnect a social media account or delete your user account:

  • All access tokens are immediately deleted
  • Scheduled posts are cancelled and deleted
  • Cached data is purged within 24 hours
  • Analytics data can be retained for up to 30 days for backup purposes

To request immediate deletion of all data, please contact us at: odo@odomarketing.com
