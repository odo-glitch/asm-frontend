Data Deletion Instructions

Odo Social Media - A product of Odo Marketing

⚠️ Important: This page explains how to delete your data from Odo Social Media. Once deleted, this action cannot be undone.

How to Delete Your Data

You have three options to delete your data from our application:

1Option 1: Disconnect Individual Accounts (In-App)

To remove a specific social media account connection:

  1. Log into your account at https://asm-frontend-omega.vercel.app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Find the Connected Accounts section
  4. Click the Disconnect button next to the account you want to remove

What gets deleted:

  • ✅ Access tokens for that account (immediately)
  • ✅ Scheduled posts for that account
  • ✅ Cached data within 24 hours

What remains:

  • Your user account
  • Other connected social media accounts

2Option 2: Delete Your Entire Account (In-App)

To completely remove your account and all associated data:

  1. Log into your account at https://asm-frontend-omega.vercel.app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to Account Management
  4. Click Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion when prompted

What gets deleted:

  • ✅ Your user account
  • ✅ All connected social media accounts
  • ✅ All access tokens (immediately)
  • ✅ All scheduled posts
  • ✅ All cached messages and content
  • ✅ All analytics data (within 30 days)

3Option 3: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access your account, you can request deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to: odo@odomarketing.com
  2. Subject line: Data Deletion Request
  3. Include:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your Facebook User ID (if available)
    • Any other identifying information

Processing time:

  • We will respond within 48 hours
  • Deletion will be completed within 7 days
  • You will receive confirmation via email

What Happens After Deletion

When you delete your data:

Data Type Deletion Timeline
Access Tokens Immediately deleted
Scheduled Posts Immediately deleted
Cached Messages Within 24 hours
Analytics Data Within 30 days
Backup Copies Within 30 days

Important Notes

  • Irreversible: Data deletion cannot be undone. You will need to reconnect and reconfigure if you want to use the app again.
  • Social Media Content: Content you posted to Facebook/Instagram through our app will remain on those platforms. We only delete data stored in our systems.
  • Legal Retention: Some data may be retained if required by law (e.g., for tax purposes or dispute resolution).
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement purposes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Will my Facebook/Instagram posts be deleted?

A: No. Content you published to Facebook or Instagram remains on those platforms. We only delete data stored in our app's database.

Q: Can I re-activate my account after deletion?

A: No. Once deleted, you would need to create a new account and reconnect your social media accounts.

Q: What if I just want to disconnect temporarily?

A: You can disconnect an account and reconnect it later. This removes access but allows you to reconnect the same account in the future.

Q: How do I know my data has been deleted?

A: You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete. You can also try logging in - deleted accounts will show an error.

For Facebook/Meta Platform Users

If you connected your Facebook Page or Instagram account through our app and want to revoke access from Facebook's side:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings
  2. Click on Apps and Websites
  3. Find our app in the list
  4. Click Remove

Note: This revokes our access but doesn't delete data from our servers. Please also follow the deletion steps above.
